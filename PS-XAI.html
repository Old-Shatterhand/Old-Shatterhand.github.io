Proseminar Explainable Aritifical Intelligence

Description

The idea of this seminar is to make the students familiar with basic algorithms in the field of artificial intelligence (AI). We will not cover neural networks, machine learning or deep learning as this is beyond the scope of this seminar. Alongside with basic AI we also want to introduce explainability. Exlainable AI (XAI) is gaining more and more importance in recent years as AI systems take over in various fields. Two prominent examples are self-driving cars and medical disgnosis. Both require lots of confidence in AI and therefore it is of huge importance to be able to understand and comprehend decisions made by AI systems. 

Other than in normal proseminars, you will get practical experience with the algorithms and implementations. You will implement one of the XAI algorithms for an AI algorithm (taken from sklearn) and present both together with the results in a 20 minutes presentation (and a ?-pages report).
